# Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Objective
This dashboard analyzes mobile sales performance across cities, brands, models, and payment methods to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

- Total Revenue: 769M
- Total Units Sold: 19K
- Total Transactions: 4K
- Revenue by City & Brand
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Payment Method Distribution
- Customer Rating Insights

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated in major metro cities.
- Certain models drive majority of sales volume.
- Weekend sales outperform weekdays.
- Digital payments dominate transaction methods.
